Capabilities

Genome Station

Addresses research demands at the nucleic acid level for microorganisms, plants, and animals

Key Functions

  • Gene synthesis
  • Metabolic pathway assembly
  • Cloning
  • DNA library construction
  • Genome scale DNA synthesis
  • Metagenome
  • DNA partbank

Molecular Station

Supports research demands at the protein, metabolic pathway, or cell-free system level

Key Functions

  • High-throughput protein/enzyme screening
  • Protein modeling
  • Protein/enzyme activity testing
  • Cell-free system-based nucleic acid/protein synthesis
  • Cell-free system-based activity testing
  • Biosensors
  • Metabolite analysis

Organism Station

Supports research demands at the cellular or fermentation level

Key Functions

  • Directed evolution
  • Fermentation condition optimization
  • Scale-up validation
