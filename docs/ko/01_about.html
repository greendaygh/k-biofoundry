About us

Development of Biofoundry DBTL Workflow, Station Construction and Integrated Operation Platform

Biofoundry is an automated research facility that aims to accelerate synthetic biology-based biotechnology research and development. Through the Design-Build-Test-Learn (DBTL) cycle, we aim to enhance the efficiency and reproducibility of biotechnology research.

01

Biofoundry Center Construction

Establishment of physical building to house and operate biofoundry automation hardware and software services

  • Total Project Cost: 54.8 billion KRW
  • Project Duration: 2024 - 2026 (3 years)
  • Building Scale: 1 basement floor, 6 above-ground floors
  • Building Area/Total Floor Area: 1,768.9 m² / 12,403.6 m²
02

DBTL Stage-specific Core Workflow Implementation

  • Design and implementation of demand-based DBTL workflows and core equipment
  • Station construction through automation equipment integration
  • Verification of station and workflow efficiency
  • Development of station operation systems
03

Biofoundry Integrated Platform Development

  • Integrated platform architecture design
  • Integrated platform database construction
  • Development of integrated platform modules for stations, workflows, support, AI, and communication
  • Development of integrated platform user manual
